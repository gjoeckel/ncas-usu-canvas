The Canvas learning management system requires users to be logged in* to take quizzes. Click on the link on the “Create a free Canvas account and sign in to take quizzes and track your progress” banner on the Core Skills page:

A new window with an “Enroll in NCADEMI Core Accessibility Skills” dialog will open:

  1. Enter your email address in the “Username” field.
  2. Select the “I am a new user” radio button.
  3. Enter your full name in the “Full Name” field.
  4. Read the Acceptable Use Policy and check the checkbox to agree.
  5. Complete the reCAPTCHA.
  6. View the Privacy Policy.
  7. Click “Enroll in Course”.

Then click on the “Go to Course” button:

*Please note: Canvas credentials from other institutions will not work.
